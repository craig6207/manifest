Cookies & Similar Technologies

About this notice

This app uses cookies and similar technologies (for example: local/device storage, app preferences, and identifiers) to operate and keep you signed in. We do not use analytics, advertising, or other non-essential technologies at this time.

“WeRecruit”, “we”, “us” refers to WeRecruit. This notice explains what we store on your device, why, and how you can control it.

What technologies we use

At present, WeRecruit uses only strictly necessary storage to operate the app: authentication and session storage on your device. No third-party SDKs are initialized.

Technology inventory

Strictly necessary (no consent required)

Access Token (access_token) Purpose: Authorise API requests and keep you signed in
  • Provider: WeRecruit (first-party, stored on device)
  • What is stored: Short-lived token string
  • When it loads: After successful login
  • Retention: Expires automatically per server settings (short-lived)
  • Legal basis: Legitimate interest / performance of contract
Refresh Token (refresh_token) Purpose: Obtain new access tokens without re-login
  • Provider: WeRecruit (first-party, stored on device)
  • What is stored: Long-lived token string
  • When it loads: After successful login
  • Retention: Persists until expiry or logout (duration set by server)
  • Legal basis: Legitimate interest / performance of contract

Non-essential technologies

We currently do not use analytics, advertising, A/B testing, or social SDKs. If this changes, we will request your consent before enabling them and update this notice.

Purposes

Run the app and secure your account (authentication, session management, fraud prevention, and security). No profiling or advertising purposes are used at this time.

Managing your choices

Because we only use strictly necessary storage, there are currently no consent toggles to manage. If we add non-essential technologies in the future, you will see an in-app privacy settings screen to grant or withdraw consent at any time.

Sharing and transfers

We do not share device-stored tokens with third parties. Tokens are transmitted securely to our backend only to authenticate you. We do not transfer these identifiers to ad partners or analytics providers.

Retention

Access tokens expire automatically after a short period. Refresh tokens persist on your device until they expire or you log out, clear app data, or uninstall the app.

Your rights

Depending on your location (e.g., UK GDPR), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict your personal data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact privacy@werecruit.example. You may also complain to your local data protection authority.

Changes to this notice

We may update this notice to reflect changes in our use of technologies. We will update the date below and, where appropriate, notify you in-app.
